Why AI skills matter now

AI is becoming a basic academic skill. Students are expected to use AI tools to research, summarize, and brainstorm. The key skill is not learning to code AI, but knowing how to ask good questions and use it responsibly. With rapid AI advancements, AI skills are no longer optional. They are becoming a core life skill, similar to writing, math, or using computers.

Smarter studying

Turn long readings into quick notes and practice questions you can review on your phone.

Sharper thinking

AI helps you brainstorm, but you still choose the best ideas and facts.

Future-ready

Graduate programs and employers want students who can use AI tools responsibly.

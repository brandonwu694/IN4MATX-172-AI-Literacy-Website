About the Project

Artificial intelligence is becoming part of everyday life and education. This project is designed to help parents, teachers, and caregivers understand what AI is, how it works, and how it can be used as a supportive learning tool rather than a replacement for human thinking.

Why AI Literacy Matters

When used responsibly, AI can support learning, creativity, and career exploration. However, AI systems can make mistakes and reflect bias. Understanding these limitations helps adults model ethical and transparent AI use for students.

🎯 Ethical Use

Learn to guide students in using AI responsibly and transparently

💡 Critical Thinking

Understand AI's limitations and how to question its outputs

🚀 Future Ready

Prepare students for a world where AI is part of everyday work
